## About me

I’m the founder of **DevHorizon Technologies Private Limited**, based in Bengaluru, India.  
I design and build high-value software systems for hospitality, construction, commerce, publishing, and service businesses.

- 🔭 Building client platforms, internal tools, and scalable digital products
- 🌱 Exploring AI-powered development, analytics, cloud architecture, and modern SaaS workflows
- 💼 Working across full-stack development, payment systems, operations software, and automation
- 🎯 Focused on premium UI/UX, business-oriented engineering, and reliable product delivery
- 📍 Bengaluru, Karnataka, India
